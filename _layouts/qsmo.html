{{ page.about-text }}

Housed in individual Federal agencies, QSMOs are charged with:

  • Offering standardized services that align to agency mission/service area
  • Integrating solutions that are interchangeable to allow for customer choice/transition
  • Committing to innovation, long-term investment, and service

Who are the QSMOs?

OMB will designate specific agencies to stand up QSMOs for select mission-support functions. These agencies will submit initial 5-Year Plans indicating how they will offer select mission-support functions to other Federal agencies. Final QSMO designation, issued by OMB in consultation with the Shared Solutions Governance Board (SSGB), is contingent upon development of the aforementioned 5-Year Plan and its alignment with the QSMO designation criteria and subsequent approval from OMB.
